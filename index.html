3 Steps to Set Up a Tunnel Tent

Stop struggling. Elegant setup techniques for solo campers.

Visual Guide

1

Thread Poles

Connect all poles. Thread them into sleeves matching color codes. Do not insert pole tips into grommets yet; keep it flat.

[Image: Pole Threading]
2

Rear Stake & Stretch

Stake down the rear corners first. Then pull the front end forward like an accordion.

Tip: Stretching downwind is easier.

3

Front Stake & Adjust

Stake the front. Finally, adjust tension on all guy lines to ensure a taut, wrinkle-free pitch.
