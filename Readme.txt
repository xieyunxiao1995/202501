### Description (应用描述 - 中文)

在 Zylotherion 的世界里，记忆是通往力量的唯一钥匙。
这是一款融合了 Roguelike 探险与记忆翻牌战斗的独特 RPG 游戏。
你将扮演一位孤独的攀塔者，在无尽的虚空之塔中不断向上。
每一次翻牌都是对记忆的考验，每一次合成都是对智慧的挑战。

游戏特色：

- 独特战斗系统：通过翻开匹配的元素卡牌来发动攻击，考验你的瞬间记忆力。
- 无尽攀塔挑战：每一层都由随机生成的敌人和事件组成，每一次冒险都独一无二。
- 深渊炼金术：收集战斗中掉落的神秘素材，在炼金实验室中融合出强大的装备。
- 极简深色美学：专为沉浸体验设计的暗色调界面，保护视力并营造神秘氛围。
- 纯粹游戏体验：无需联网，没有广告，没有复杂的账号系统，打开即玩。

准备好挑战你的记忆极限了吗？Zylotherion 等待着你的攀登。

### Description (应用描述 - English)

In the world of Zylotherion, memory is the only key to power.
This is a unique RPG that blends Roguelike exploration with memory card matching combat.
You play as a lone climber, ascending the endless Tower of Void.
Every card flip is a test of memory, and every synthesis is a challenge of wit.

Game Features:

- Unique Combat System: Attack enemies by flipping matching elemental cards, testing your instant memory.
- Endless Tower Challenge: Each floor is procedurally generated with random enemies and events.
- Abyssal Alchemy: Collect mysterious materials dropped in battle and fuse them into powerful gear in the Alchemy Lab.
- Minimalist Dark Aesthetics: Dark-themed interface designed for immersion and eye comfort.
- Pure Gaming Experience: No internet required, no ads, no complex account system. Just play.

Are you ready to test the limits of your memory? Zylotherion awaits your ascent.
